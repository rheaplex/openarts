the artistic advantages of copyleft

Copyleft, or Open Art, trades the protection of revenue by copyright licensing for the protection of reputation by copyright licensing. Using Open Art places existing work at your disposal, so you gain more than you lose.

Open art, has several advantages for both established and XXXXX artists:

  • It helps you make collaborative works.
  • It helps distribution and reproduction of your works, giving exposure.
  • It allows you to use work by other artists.
  • It does not prevent you selling copies of the work.

There is a long tradition of sharing work and variations on work in the arts. EXAMPLES.
